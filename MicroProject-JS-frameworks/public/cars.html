2017 Hyundai Elantra

The 2017 Hyundai Elantra offers a blend of sleek design and cutting-edge features at an affordable price. With its smooth drive, modern technology, and exceptional fuel efficiency, the Elantra is perfect for city commutes and weekend getaways. Packed with safety features such as automatic emergency braking, lane-keeping assist, and a rearview camera, this car ensures a safe and reliable ride. Plus, with its spacious interior and user-friendly infotainment system, it’s a great choice for families.

  • Engine: 2.0L 4-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 147 hp
  • Fuel Economy: 26 MPG city / 36 MPG highway
  • Price: $13,500

2023 Fisker Ocean

Introducing the 2023 Fisker Ocean – a fully-electric SUV that redefines sustainability without compromising on luxury. Designed with the environment in mind, the Ocean is built from recycled materials, including a vegan leather interior. With a range of up to 350 miles on a single charge, the Fisker Ocean ensures you can drive further while minimizing your carbon footprint. This stylish electric SUV also comes equipped with advanced technology, including an intuitive infotainment system, autonomous driving features, and a spacious interior. Elevate your driving experience while contributing to a greener planet!

  • Engine: Dual-motor electric
  • Horsepower: 540 hp
  • Range: 350 miles (on a full charge)
  • Price: $37,499

2020 Tesla Model 3

The 2020 Tesla Model 3 combines stunning performance, cutting-edge technology, and a minimalist design to create the ultimate electric vehicle. With acceleration that rivals sports cars and a range of up to 358 miles, the Model 3 offers unmatched driving pleasure. The car’s Autopilot features provide a semi-autonomous driving experience, while the large touchscreen display controls almost every function of the car. Whether you’re looking for a reliable daily driver or a high-tech adventure, the Model 3 is sure to impress.

  • Engine: Electric (Dual-motor all-wheel drive)
  • Horsepower: 283 hp (Standard Range Plus)
  • Range: 358 miles (on a full charge)
  • Price: $35,000
